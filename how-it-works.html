How Miniverse works

Miniverse is a scroll feed of 18 free browser mini games — similar to short-video apps, but every card is something you can play instantly.

The feed

Swipe or scroll vertically to move between games. Each card fills most of the screen so you can focus on one experience at a time. Use the dots on the right edge to see how far you are in the feed.

For You vs Games

For You highlights our curated mix — stress relief, daily rituals, and fan favorites like Squishy Chick and Neon Rush.

Games lists the full arcade and puzzle collection. Filter by Arcade, Puzzle, Chill, or Social to narrow the list, or open a category hub for a browsable landing page:

Learn is the knowledge tab — a news-style feed of play tips, strategy articles, and guide highlights. Search, filter by topic, and save articles for later.

  • ⚡ Arcade & action — runners, rhythm, reflex
  • 🧩 Puzzle — merge, match, stack
  • 🌸 Chill — cozy, daily rituals, low pressure
  • ✨ Social — memes, glow-ups, share cards

Deep link example: index.html?cat=arcade opens the Games tab with Arcade filtered.

Search & discovery

Type in the search bar to filter games by name, or press / on desktop to focus search instantly. Category chips under the intro bar link to hub pages with play buttons and guides for every game in that vibe.

Today's pick & what's new

The button below the search bar rotates daily with a suggested game and mini challenge — for example, reaching 2048 on the classic slide puzzle. The tip beside it highlights a recent feature; tap through to the full changelog.

Saved favorites

Tap the star icon on any game card to save it. Open ⭐ in the top navigation to see your personal list with quick links back to each game and its guide.

Share cards

After playing, look for Share buttons on result screens, toolbars, or reward panels. Miniverse generates image cards for fortunes, scores, quests, check-ins, memes, and streaks — save them or post to chats and stories.

Daily vibe streak

Play at least one game per calendar day to grow your streak. The flame banner at the top tracks your count locally — no account needed. Tap 📤 on the streak bar to share when you are on a roll.

Privacy-friendly by design

No account is required. Progress, streaks, and favorites are stored locally in your browser. We do not sell personal data. See our Privacy Policy for details.

FAQ

Do I need to download anything?
No — all games run in the browser. You can add Miniverse to your home screen via the web app manifest on mobile.
Are the games free?
Yes. All 18 mini games are free to play. The site may show ads when enabled.
Where are guides?
Every game has a how-to-play page in Game guides with tips and FAQ.
Can I pick games by mood?
Yes — see Play tips for recommendations by category and break length.
