⭐ Starfall

Move your basket across the night sky, catch glowing stars, and dodge meteors — classic arcade reflex in a cozy wrapper.

Catch stars, dodge meteors, share the high score.
▶ Play Starfall now

Who is this for?

Arcade fans who like simple controls and escalating intensity. Short runs fit commutes.

How to play

Start and move the basket left/right (D-pad or drag). Stars add score; meteors end the run. Share score on the pause screen.

Tips

  • Stay centered until meteors telegraph a lane.
  • Rhythm of stars speeds up — stay calm.
  • Share every personal best from Star Pause.

FAQ

Controls?
D-pad buttons or touch drag.
End condition?
Meteor hit ends the run.
Share?
Share score on Star Pause screen.
