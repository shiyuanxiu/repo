🀄 Tile Draw

Solo random tile draws with zero rules — tap the deck, reveal a tile, repeat. Digital ASMR for puzzle fans who want no pressure.

Infinite draws, zero pressure — digital tile zen.

Who is this for?

Players seeking pure chill with no fail state. Not for those wanting competitive scoring.

How to play

Tap the deck to reveal tiles one at a time. Counter tracks draws this session. Exit anytime — no penalty.

Tips

  • Use between intense arcade rounds to reset.
  • Great one-handed mobile pastime.
  • No timer — stop whenever you want.
