🍜 Mochi Snake · Food Hunt

A plump white-and-pink mochi caterpillar slithers through a hand-painted meadow. Tap to steer toward snacks, grow longer, and dodge colorful rival snakes that wander in from the grass.

Steamer & noodles 1pt · Spring roll & mooncake 5pt · Roast duck 10pt — more food appears as your score climbs.
▶ Play Mochi Snake now

Who is this for?

Classic snake fans who want cozy watercolor vibes instead of neon grids. One-thumb tap steering on mobile — no D-pad needed. Great for a quick five-minute score chase.

How to play

  1. Tap ▶ Start on the feed card.
  2. Tap or drag anywhere on the meadow — your snake turns toward your finger on the dominant axis.
  3. Eat food to score and grow. Higher-value snacks are rarer but worth the detour.
  4. Avoid meadow edges, your own tail, and rival snakes (yellow, purple, green).
  5. New rivals spawn over time — watch for the red pulse when one appears.

Food guide

  • 1 pt — Steamer buns, noodle bowls, rice boxes (common)
  • 5 pt — Spring rolls, mooncakes (uncommon, gold ring)
  • 10 pt — Roast duck (rare, bright gold ring)

Tips

  • Early rounds stay calm — only a handful of snacks on the field until you score more.
  • Think two moves ahead — speed increases every ~20 points.
  • Grab 10pt duck only when you have a clear escape route.
  • Use Full on the card for a taller meadow on small screens.
  • Share your score from the game-over overlay after a good run.

FAQ

Food values?
Common snacks 1pt, spring rolls and mooncakes 5pt, roast duck 10pt.
Controls?
Tap or drag on the meadow canvas — the snake steers toward your pointer.
Rival snakes?
Up to five rivals roam the meadow. Touching one ends your run instantly.
Share?
Tap Share score on the game-over screen, or use the feed share button.
