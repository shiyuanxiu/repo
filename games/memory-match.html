🃏 Memory Match

Flip cards to find matching pairs and clear the board. Train memory with a calm pace — no harsh timer.

Seaside pairs — your move count is the flex.
▶ Play offline (single file) ▶ Play in feed

Who is this for?

Puzzle players of all ages who like brain warm-ups without arcade stress.

How to play

Tap cards to flip two at a time. Matching seaside pairs stay revealed; mismatches flip back with a gentle shake. Win when all pairs are found — fewer moves beats your best score.

Difficulty

  • Easy — 3×2 grid (3 pairs)
  • Medium — 4×3 grid (6 pairs)
  • Hard — 4×4 grid (8 pairs)

Tips

  • Memorize corners and edges first.
  • Plan the second flip before revealing the first.
  • Fewer moves means a better run — share to challenge friends.

FAQ

Time limit?
No hard timer — focus on move efficiency.
Board size?
Choose Easy (3×2), Medium (4×3), or Hard (4×4).
Share?
