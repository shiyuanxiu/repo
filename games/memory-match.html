🃏 Memory Match

Flip cards to find matching pairs and clear the board. Train memory with a calm pace — no harsh timer.

Six pairs, your move count is the flex.
▶ Play Memory Match now

Who is this for?

Puzzle players of all ages who like brain warm-ups without arcade stress.

How to play

Tap cards to flip two at a time. Matching pairs stay revealed; mismatches flip back. Win when all pairs are found — share your move count.

Tips

  • Memorize corners and edges first.
  • Plan the second flip before revealing the first.
  • Fewer moves means a better run — share to challenge friends.

FAQ

Time limit?
No hard timer — focus on move efficiency.
Board size?
6 pairs in standard mode.
Share?
Share result on All pairs screen.
