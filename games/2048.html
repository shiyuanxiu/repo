2048 — Classic slide puzzle

Gabriele Cirulli’s original rules: swipe the whole 4×4 board, merge matching numbers, reach the 2048 tile.

▶ Play 2048

Rules

  • 4×4 grid; two random tiles at start (90% are 2, 10% are 4).
  • Swipe up/down/left/right — all tiles slide that way and merge once per move.
  • Example: 2,2,2 shifted right becomes empty, 2, 4 (not 8).
  • A new tile appears only after a move that changed the board.
  • Score increases by the value of each merge (2+2 → +4).
  • Win at 2048; you can keep playing. Lose when the board is full with no merges.
