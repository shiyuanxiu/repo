🌸 Daily Fortune

Draw a daily fortune stick online. Tap the tube, reveal a kindness prompt, and share your result — a calm ritual for starting or ending the day.

Not fortune-telling — a kindness nudge you can act on today.
▶ Play Daily Fortune now

Who is this for?

Perfect for mindfulness fans and anyone wanting a gentle daily nudge. Not a predictive fortune-telling service — prompts are kind suggestions.

How to play

Tap the fortune tube on the card. After the animation, read your prompt on the scroll. Draw Again for another line, or Share fortune to save a card.

Tips

  • Try one fortune action per day for a simple habit.
  • Share cards work great for morning social posts.
  • Pair with Blue Planet Diary for real-world quests.

FAQ

Is this real fortune telling?
No — light-hearted daily prompts for self-care.
Can I draw multiple times?
Yes, use Draw Again anytime.
Share support?
Built-in Share fortune button on results.
