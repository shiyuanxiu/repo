Who is this for?

Merge puzzle veterans and newcomers alike. Perfect for thoughtful breaks.

How to play

Swipe or use arrow keys to slide tiles. Equal numbers merge into doubles. Game ends when the grid is full — share your score and highest tile.

Tips

  • Anchor your largest tile in a corner.
  • Avoid random up/down swipes — pick two primary directions.
  • Share when you hit a new tile milestone.
