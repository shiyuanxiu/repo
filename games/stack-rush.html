🐷 Piggy Catch

Sheep-a-Sheep style puzzle by the river. A pink pig catches fish and shells — match three of a kind before the 7 slots fill up.

▶ Play Piggy Catch now

How to play

Tap any uncovered tile on the water. It moves into the tray below (max 7 slots). When 3 identical items sit in the tray, they vanish. Clear every tile to win the level. If the tray fills with no match, game over.

Items

Clownfish, golden fish, blue fish, puffer fish, seahorse, white shell, striped shell, pearl shell.

Tips

  • Only the top layer of each stack is tappable — plan ahead.
  • Same-type items group together in the tray when picked.
  • Later levels add more tile sets — stay calm and match steadily.
