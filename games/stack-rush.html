🧱 Stack Rush

Drop sliding blocks onto a tower — misaligned edges get sliced off until the tower falls. Pure timing satisfaction.

Misaligned edges get sliced until the tower falls.
▶ Play Stack Rush now

Who is this for?

Fans of stackers like the viral tower genre. One-button gameplay, high retry value.

How to play

Tap or press Space to drop each block when aligned over the tower. Count stacked levels until collapse. Share level count when the tower falls.

Tips

  • Watch block speed — it stays steady per level.
  • After a bad drop, play safer with smaller overhang.
  • Share your best level count after each fall.

FAQ
