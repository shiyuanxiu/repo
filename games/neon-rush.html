⚡ Neon Rush

A three-lane cyber endless runner in your browser. Swap lanes, jump, slide, collect coins, and chase distance — built for quick adrenaline breaks.

Three lanes, one distance number worth chasing.
▶ Play Neon Rush now

Who is this for?

Ideal for reflex lovers and high-score chasers. Less suited if you want zero-fail chill vibes.

How to play

Tap Start Run, then use on-screen D-pad or arrow keys: left/right to change lanes, up to jump, down to slide. Avoid obstacles and survive as long as you can.

Tips

  • Look ahead — obstacles telegraph early in each lane.
  • Slide under low barriers instead of jumping late.
  • Share your score from the Run Over screen.

FAQ

Is Neon Rush free?
Yes, free in your browser.
Keyboard support?
Arrow keys work on desktop.
Can I share scores?
Yes — tap Share score after a run.
Mobile controls?
On-screen D-pad buttons.
