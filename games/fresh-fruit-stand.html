🏪 Fresh Fruit Stand

Run a cozy corner fruit shop in 60-second rounds. Restock shelves, serve customers, and maximize revenue — light tycoon vibes in the browser.

Tycoon lite: restock, serve, count your revenue.
▶ Play Fresh Fruit Stand now

Who is this for?

Fans of casual shop sims and time-management lite. Good for players who like clear goals each round.

How to play

Open Shop, restock by tapping goods then empty shelves. When customers reach checkout, tap to complete sales. Round ends after 60 seconds — review revenue and rating.

Tips

  • Keep popular shelves full early in the round.
  • Watch customer paths to prioritize restocks.
  • Share your revenue from Round Complete.
