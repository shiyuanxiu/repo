What's new

Miniverse ships small improvements often — new share cards, clearer guides, and better ways to find your next mini game. Here is a running log of recent highlights.

▶ Open the game feed

May 2026

  • Share cards everywhere — Every game with a score or result screen now has a Share button. Fortune, memes, quests, check-ins, streaks, and arcade scores all export image cards.
  • Category hub pages — Browse Arcade, Puzzle, Chill, and Social collections with play links and guides.
  • Deep guide refresh — All 18 game guides rebuilt with who-it's-for, tips, FAQ, and structured data for search engines.
  • Feed search — Press / on desktop or use the search bar to filter games by name instantly.
  • Trust polish — Removed fake comment counts and placeholder avatars from the feed for a cleaner, honest experience.

Earlier highlights

  • Daily vibe streak — Play once per day to grow your streak; share a streak card from the feed banner.
  • Saved favorites — Star any game and revisit from the Saved page.
  • Today's pick — A rotating daily challenge on the feed intro bar.
  • PWA manifest — Add Miniverse to your home screen on mobile for app-like launches.
  • Cookie consent — Transparent cookie banner aligned with privacy policy and AdSense readiness.

Coming next

We are exploring per-game preview images for social sharing, more editorial roundups, and optional category filters on the Saved page. Have a request? Contact us.
