Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 30, 2026

Miniverse (“we”, “our”, “us”) operates miniverse.gg and related pages offering free browser games and interactive content. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information when you use our site.

Information we collect

Information stored on your device

  • Local storage: Game progress, daily streaks, saved favorites, and similar preferences are stored in your browser. We do not automatically receive this data.
  • Cookie consent: If you accept cookies on our site, we store your choice locally.

Information collected automatically

  • Server logs: Our hosting provider may record IP address, browser type, referring URL, and pages visited for security and performance.
  • Analytics & advertising partners: When enabled, partners such as Google AdSense may use cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies to serve ads, measure ad performance, and limit repeat ads.

Google AdSense & third-party advertising

We may use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google and its partners may:

  • Use cookies to serve ads based on your visits to this and other websites.
  • Collect device and usage data as described in How Google uses data when you use our partners' sites or apps.
  • Provide personalized or non-personalized ads depending on your region and settings.

You can manage ad personalization:

  • Google Ads Settings
  • aboutads.info (US)
  • Your Online Choices (EU)

See also Google Privacy Policy and Google partner sites policy.

Legal bases (EEA / UK users)

Where GDPR applies, we rely on:

  • Consent for non-essential cookies and personalized advertising (via our cookie banner where shown).
  • Legitimate interests for site security, basic analytics, and serving non-personalized ads when consent is not given.

How we use information

  • Operate games and improve the feed experience
  • Display and measure advertisements
  • Prevent fraud, abuse, and invalid ad activity
  • Respond to support and legal requests

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with service providers (hosting, advertising) as needed to run the site, or when required by law.

Children

Miniverse is for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or 16 in some regions). Contact privacy@miniverse.gg if you believe a child provided personal data.

Your choices

  • Clear browser local storage to reset game data and favorites.
  • Block or delete cookies in browser settings (some features or ads may not work correctly).
  • Use the links above to opt out of personalized advertising.
  • Email privacy@miniverse.gg for privacy-related requests.

Changes

We may update this policy. The “Last updated” date will change when we do. Continued use after changes means you accept the updated policy.
