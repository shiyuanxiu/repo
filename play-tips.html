Play tips

Miniverse is built for short breaks — one swipe, one game, no download. Pick by mood, browse category hubs, or use the feed search bar to jump straight to a title.

Best games for a 1-minute break

These load instantly and do not punish you for closing the tab: Squishy Chick, Daily Fortune, Brain Sketch, Born To vs Forced To, and Mystery Flavor Box. Perfect between meetings or on the bus.

How the vibe streak works

Play at least one game per calendar day to grow your streak. The flame banner at the top of the feed shows your current count. Tap 📤 on the streak bar to share a streak card when you are on a roll. Data stays in your browser — no account required.

Find games faster

  • Use the search bar on the feed to filter all 18 games by name.
  • Press / on desktop to jump to search.
  • Switch to the Games tab and filter Arcade / Puzzle / Chill / Social.
  • Tap ⭐ on any game to save it — open Saved from the navbar.
  • Read detailed how-to-play pages in All game guides.

Sharing your results

Every game with a score or result screen now includes a Share button. Fortune, memes, quests, check-ins, streaks, and arcade scores export image cards you can save or post. See what's new for the full list.
